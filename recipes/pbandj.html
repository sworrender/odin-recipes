Peanut Butter and Jelly

Recipe for Peanut butter and Jelly

Ingredients

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Lay two pieces of bread flat on a plate
  2. Using a knife spead peanut butter on one side of the bread
  3. using another knife spread jelly on the other side of the bread
  4. combine both sides of the bread with the peanut butter and jelly on the inside touching each other
